Solutions of Obesity

Top 3 Solutions of Obesity: Eat better Foods

  • Eating healthy is one of the best ways to manage obesity.
  • Try to eat more:
    • Fruits
    • Vegetables
    • Whole grains
    • Foods like chicken, eggs, or beans
  • Healthy foods give your body energy without too much sugar or fat.
  • Limit junk food such as:
    • Chips
    • Candy
    • Fast food
    • Soda
  • You don’t need to be perfect—just make better choices most of the time.
  • Small changes can help a lot, for example:
    • Have fruit instead of cookies
    • Drink water instead of soda

    • Move more

  • Being active every day is very important.
  • You don’t need to play a sport or go to a gym—just find fun ways to move your body.
  • Ways to move your body:
    • Dance
    • Ride your bike
    • Take a walk
    • Play outside
    • Do chores that get you moving
  • Try to get at least 30 to 60 minutes of activity each day.
  • Moving your body helps:
    • Burn extra energy (calories)
    • Make muscles and heart stronger
    • Feel happier and less stressed
  • Small changes can also help, like:
    • Walking instead of riding in a car
    • Taking the stairs

    • Ask for help

  • You don’t have to face obesity alone—it’s okay to ask for help.
  • Talk to someone you trust, such as:
    • A parent
    • School nurse
    • Teacher
    • Doctor
  • They can give advice and help you set simple goals to get healthier.
  • Doing activities with a friend or family member can help, for example:
    • Going for walks
    • Cooking healthy meals together
  • Having support makes it easier to stay motivated.
  • Start with small steps, like:
    • Drinking more water
    • Taking a short walk
  • Every little step counts!
