Effects of Obesity

Top 3 Effects of Obesity: Health Problems

Being overweight can make kids sick. It can cause:

  • Diabetes
  • High blood pressure
  • Heart problems
  • Too much body fat can make it hard to breathe and makes the heart work harder.
  • Problems can get worse if not treated.
  • Ways to stay healthy:
  • Eat fruits and vegetables
  • Play and move every day
  • Go to the doctor for check-ups

Joint and Body Pain

Carrying extra weight can hurt your bones and joints. It can cause pain in your:

  • Knees
  • Hips
  • Lower back
  • Extra weight makes walking, running, and playing sports harder.
  • Over time, joints can get damaged, and problems like arthritis can happen.
  • When movement hurts, it’s easy to stop being active, which can make the pain worse.
  • Staying active and keeping a healthy weight helps your joints.
  • Exercise helps by:
  • Making muscles stronger
  • Reducing pressure on bones
  • Making daily activities easier and less painful

    • Feelings and Mental Health

  • Being overweight can affect your feelings.
  • Some kids may feel unhappy with how they look.
  • Being teased or left out can make you feel sad or worried.
  • It can make school and time with friends less fun.
  • Remember: Your weight does not decide who you are. You are special just as you are! Ways to feel better:
  • Talk to someone you trust, like a parent, teacher, or counselor
  • Make healthy choices for your body and mind
